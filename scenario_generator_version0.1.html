Welcome to the scenario library

Scenario exercises enable participants to envision and analyze the future through detailed stories of how the future might unfold. Scenarios were popularized in military operations in the 1950s, and are now used routinely in emergency management, governments, nonprofits, and corporations.

Most commonly, scenarios present an extreme or hypothetical situation that tests how people collaborate across organizations and handle emergencies. Major outcomes include collaboration, learning, development of new skills, and improvements to plans and protocols.

This tool will generate customized disaster scenarios based on real scientific data. You will get to personalize each scenario based on your local situation and interests.

This is a demonstration tool. We are excited to receive your feedback.
